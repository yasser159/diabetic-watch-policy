Diabetic Watch Privacy Policy

1. Who we are

Diabetic Watch is a glucose logging application operated by the app publisher for the repository at github.com/yasser159/diabetic_watch_react. Questions about this policy can be sent to yasser159@gmail.com.

2. Information we handle

Information you provide directly

  • Email address and password if you create an account with email sign-in.
  • Basic account identifiers returned by the authentication provider, such as user ID and email address.
  • Blood glucose readings you enter, including value, timestamp, unit, reading slot, and optional notes.
  • Optional feedback you send through sharing or support channels.

Information generated when you use the app

  • Reminder scheduling information used to create local meal reminder notifications on your device.
  • Local settings and preferences such as language selection and in-app reminder state.
  • Structured diagnostics and error logs that help detect failures and troubleshoot bugs.

Third-party service data

  • Authentication and backend records handled through Firebase services used by the app.
  • If Google Sign-In is used, Google account authentication data needed to complete sign-in.
  • If error tracking is enabled in a given build, limited technical diagnostics may be sent to the configured error tracking service.
Diabetic Watch is designed for logging and educational trend review. It is not intended to provide a medical diagnosis or treatment plan.

3. How we use information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To store, sync, and display your glucose readings across supported sessions and devices.
  • To generate trends, summaries, educational insights, and printable/shareable reports.
  • To schedule meal reminder notifications that you request.
  • To diagnose bugs, monitor reliability, and improve the app.
  • To respond to support or deletion requests.

4. How information may be shared

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only in the following limited situations:

  • With service providers that operate the app infrastructure, such as Firebase authentication, database, and serverless function services.
  • With external apps or services when you choose to export, print, email, text, or share a report.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the service.

5. Data retention

  • Account and glucose data are retained until you delete them, delete your account, or request deletion.
  • Some local app settings may remain on your device until the app is removed or app data is cleared.
  • Operational logs are retained only as long as reasonably needed for diagnostics, security, and service maintenance.

6. Your choices and rights

  • You can review and manage much of your data directly inside the app.
  • You can delete your account from the app’s Settings screen.
  • You can also request account deletion or privacy assistance by emailing yasser159@gmail.com.
  • You can manage notification permissions in your device settings.

For deletion instructions, visit the Account Deletion page.

7. Children’s privacy

Diabetic Watch is not intended for children under 13 without parental or guardian involvement. If you believe a child has provided personal information inappropriately, contact us so we can review and remove it when required.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. International processing

Depending on the infrastructure used by the app, your information may be processed or stored in countries other than your own. By using the app, you understand that these transfers may occur where permitted by law.

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made. Material changes should also be reflected in the app store listings and in-app links where appropriate.

11. Contact

Privacy questions, deletion requests, or support issues can be sent to yasser159@gmail.com.
