Account Deletion

Delete your Diabetic Watch account

Effective date: March 9, 2026

You can delete your Diabetic Watch account directly inside the mobile app or request deletion by email.

Back to policy hub Privacy Policy

Option 1: Delete your account in the app

  1. Open Diabetic Watch and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open the Delete account section.
  4. Type DELETE to confirm.
  5. Submit the deletion request.

When deletion succeeds, the app is designed to remove your cloud-stored profile and readings associated with your account and then delete the authentication account.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email yasser159@gmail.com from the address associated with your account and include:

  • The email address used with Diabetic Watch
  • A short statement that you want your account deleted

What is deleted

  • Your app account and authentication record
  • Your cloud-stored glucose readings and profile records associated with that account

What may remain

  • Limited records retained when required by law, security, fraud prevention, or operational integrity
  • Information you already exported or shared outside the app
  • Local data that still exists on your device until the app is removed or device storage is cleared

For broader details about data handling, see the Privacy Policy.
